About Me...

Driven by curiosity, resilience, and creativity, I’ve built a dynamic journey across marketing, hospitality, and the textile industry. My professional path began early volunteering at Banco Alimentar and Fundação Ronald McDonald taught me the power of teamwork, while working in restaurants and events at 16y instilled adaptability and responsibility. In 2020, I pivoted towards my passion, pursuing a Marketing Management degree at IPAM-Porto and interning at Quinta Nova de Nossa Senhora do Carmo, Lda., where I gained hands-on experience in brand strategy and innovation. Later, at Trotinete, I applied my strategic mindset to the textile industry, developing skills in project management and creative execution in a fast-paced environment. Now, I’m back in the world of wine at Quinta do Cume reconnecting with an industry that has always inspired me and where I continue to grow professionally. Outside of work, I find inspiration in outdoor adventures, live music, and creative pursuits. To me, every challenge is an opportunity to grow and I’m just getting started.
